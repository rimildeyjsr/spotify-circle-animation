Projects

Cinematic (Project Motion)

Cinematic is an Android App that fetches details about popular, upcoming, and trending movies and showcases it to users. The app also contains reviews for the movie, ratings for the movie, release date and the trailers which can be watched.

Cinematic

Walldo

Walldo is an Android Wallpaper app that serves users beautiful wallpapers from Unsplash. It queries the Unsplash API to get the catalog of images. Users can set an image as Home Screen or Lock Screen Wallpaper

Walldo

Pantheon 2016

Pantheon is a companion app for the bi-annual fesitval held at RNS Institute of technology. The app helps the attendees to keep a track of event schedules, venues, details and coordinators contact numbers. It implements push notifications to provide important alerts regarding the fesitval.

Pantheon 2016

GST Tracker

GST Tracker is a simple Android app built to help the common man to find out the Goods and Service Tax rates on several goods imposed by the Government of India. The app has search functionality and filters to narrow down the search to specific goods.

GST Tracker

Stock Hawk

Stock Hawk is an Android app to help users keep track of stocks and their trade history. It uses the Yahoo Finance API to fetch the stock details and it periodically refreshes them. The app has multiple accessibility features like TalkBack to help the differently abled users.

Stock Hawk
